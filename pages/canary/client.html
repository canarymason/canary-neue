Our Work

Media relations and marketing campaign

Canary Promotion is working with the Philadelphia Art Alliance to promote its transformative fall centerpiece exhibition that turns the former Rittenhouse Square mansion into a three-story installation reflecting on family life and notions of home, the shifting culture of contemporary China, and the stories objects hold. Our role in this world premiere exhibition includes media relations, social media strategy and relationship-building among partners and supporters.
